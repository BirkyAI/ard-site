## Market Overview

The Guatemalan real estate market, particularly in Antigua Guatemala, has seen steady growth over the past few years. Foreign buyers are increasingly discovering the value proposition that Antigua offers.

## Property Prices

- **Land**: $30-100/m² depending on location
- **Colonial homes**: $150,000-800,000+ in the historic center
- **Modern construction**: $80,000-300,000 outside the center
- **Luxury properties**: $500,000-2,000,000+

## Legal Process

Buying property in Guatemala as a foreigner is straightforward:
1. Find a property you love
2. Hire a lawyer to conduct due diligence
3. Sign a purchase agreement
4. Complete the transfer at the property registry
5. The entire process typically takes 30-60 days

## Investment Potential

Antigua offers excellent investment potential:
- Growing tourism driving rental demand
- Property values appreciating 5-10% annually
- Strong Airbnb market with 8-15% yields
- Limited supply in the historic center
