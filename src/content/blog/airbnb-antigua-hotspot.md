## The US Market Problem

The US Airbnb market has become increasingly saturated. Competition is fierce, regulations are tightening, and yields are declining. Many investors are looking for alternatives.

## Why Antigua Guatemala?

Antigua Guatemala offers everything an Airbnb investor dreams of:
- **Growing tourism**: 500,000+ visitors annually
- **Limited supply**: The historic center has a fixed number of properties
- **High demand**: Year-round tourism with peak seasons
- **Low operating costs**: Property management, maintenance, and utilities are affordable

## Real Numbers

Based on actual properties in Antigua:
- **Average nightly rate**: $80-150 for a colonial home
- **Occupancy rate**: 60-80% annually
- **Annual rental income**: $18,000-45,000
- **Net yield**: 8-15% after expenses

## Best Areas for Airbnb

1. **Antigua Centro** — Highest demand, premium rates
2. **Paseo del Obispo** — Quiet, popular with families
3. **San Miguel Dueñas** — Growing area, better value
