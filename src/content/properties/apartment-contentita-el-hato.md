### Why El Hato?

El Hato is a peaceful mountain village just above Antigua Guatemala, known for its cooler climate, stunning volcano views, and lush forested hillsides. It's the perfect escape from the bustle of Antigua while remaining only a short drive from the city's restaurants, shops, and cultural attractions. Ideal for those seeking tranquility, nature, and a truly unique living experience.
