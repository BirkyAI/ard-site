## About This Studio

**Studio Creativa** is a charming rustic nature studio within the exclusive **Finca El Tambor** in El Hato. Enjoy views of the nature reserve and surrounding volcanoes from this spacious studio, where natural light fills the space and brings a sense of calm throughout the day. Quiet and inviting, this studio has been a favorite long-term retreat for artists and writers working on novels, screenplays, and creative projects.

The studio features a queen-size bed, an en-suite bathroom with a shower, and a private balcony/patio. Conveniently located near the café and restaurant on the property.

### Why El Hato?

El Hato is a peaceful mountain village just above Antigua Guatemala, known for its cooler climate, stunning volcano views, and lush forested hillsides. It's the perfect escape from the bustle of Antigua while remaining only a short drive from the city's restaurants, shops, and cultural attractions. Ideal for those seeking tranquility, nature, and a truly unique living experience.
